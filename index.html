Who are we?

Live Well Care Village is a well-established care provider centre with its headquarters in Durban, South Africa. We offer professional services dedicated to the well-being and comfort of adults and children, ensuring a safe and supportive environment. Our operations are aligned with the highest standards of care, and we are fully registered with relevant health and safety authorities, adhering to all regulations to provide exceptional services to our community.

Who do we serve?

We're dedicated to providing efficient and compassionate care services tailored to the needs of all our residents. We take pride in offering personalized, cost-effective solutions to ensure the comfort, safety, and well-being of all our residents, whether in independent or assisted living.
